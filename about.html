SecureWin

About

This application 'SecureWin' aims to revolutionize the lottery system in Kerala by introducing a secure and transparent process that addresses issues such as fraud, lack of winner acknowledgment, and the misuse of lottery winnings.

Key Features

  • Secure Process: Utilizes mobile OTP verification for lottery sellers, ensuring buyer information security.
  • Automatic Notifications: Winners are alerted automatically after the results are published.
  • Fraud Prevention: Empowers officials to search for winners and prevent fraudulent activities.
  • Data Reset: Application resets data after winners are acknowledged and rewarded.
